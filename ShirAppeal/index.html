Spring 2013

Shir Appeal had a wonderful time touring the San Francisco and the Bay Area this January! They are now back at Tufts and ready for a fantastic spring semester. Amidst the snow and cold, Shir Appeal will be singing away the Winter madness and welcoming Spring back on the Hill with their Spring Show on April 13th at the Tufts Hillel Center!

Looking for even more talent, Shir Appeal brought two more singers into the group, and we are so excited to welcome Dani Bennett (Class of 2015) and Noa Rosen (Class of 2016) to our ranks!

Now, Shir Appeal is ready to continue the year with even more upcoming performances in the coming weeks and months! Check out our shows page to follow Shir Appeal in action. Check out our media page to see Shir Appeal singing in the most unlikely of places, as well as a recording of Shir Appeal singing Leonard Cohen's Hallelujah, filmed and edited by Amir Mosallaie!

About us

Shir Appeal, Tufts University's ONLY Co-ed, Jewish A Cappella group has had almost two decades of experience, singing along the spectrum of Jewish music - from Hebrew Pop, Israeli Rock, Traditional/ Liturgical melodies and even English songs with Jewish themes.

Based at Tufts University, Shir Appeal hosts many annual events at Tufts' Hillel. Stop by and pick up our annual demo CD!

Feel free to email us at shirappeal@gmail.com for information on booking, performance schedule, or ordering CDs. We'd love to hear from you!
