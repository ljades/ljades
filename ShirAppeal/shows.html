Tour info

Each year Shir Appeal hits the road and takes a national tour. Our past destinations include Southern California, Florida, NYC/Philly, and the San Francisco Bay area. Typically these tours happen early January. This year, we crossed the country to spread our music to the West Coast, as Shir Appeal returned to the San Francisco Bay Area by popular request!


Booking

Interested in booking Shir Appeal for your next event? We perform at a wide variety of venues, from birthdays to benefits to bar mitzvahs. Contact our Business Manager Michael at shirappeal@gmail.com for more information.
